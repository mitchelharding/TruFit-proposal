VIRTUAL TRAINING TECHNOLOGY

  • A VIRTUAL TRAINER: A real trainer who checks in on the client daily/weekly to make sure they are getting their meals in and doing the workouts as perscribed. They will be there to answer any questions the client has regarding movement and nutritional standards as well as motivational support for the duration of the program.
  • A TRAINING MANUAL: A user-manual for the human body. This is intended to teach the client about how their body was intended to move and perform. The workouts can be sent out weekly, monthly depending on the subscription model decided upon.
  • A NUTRTITIONAL PLAN: A nutrition plan structured to guide the client through the duration of of their training. It should offer a calculated measurement system thats intuitive and easy to follow. The structure of the Nutrtion guide should depend on subscription model: Either one full self-scalable guide or a weekly version thats scaled to their body type and dietary needs/restrictions.
